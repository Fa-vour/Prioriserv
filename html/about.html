WHO WE ARE

Prioriserv Consults Ltd is a leading indigenous Nigerian company incorporated in 2018 under the Law & Allied matter Act of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (RC 1512080).

We at Prioriserv recognise that our success stems from leaders who are knowledgeable, respected, and trustworthy, working with employees who exemplify pride, initiative, and personal conviction. Through these high ideals, we lead as an entity with genuine concern for our employees and clients, making them a top priority. The resulting loyalty and sense of honour elevate a greatness that is measured by the achievements of our employees.

We provide innovative solutions that span the entire field from construction to production facilities.

Since 2018, we have been at the front position in delivering engineering, procurement, training and construction projects within the oil and gas industry and education establishments. From Engineering and construction upgrade Projects to procurement of several topside equipment, we have provided the technology to meet customers’ valuable resources (plants, facilities, and infrastructure).

Prioriserv Consults Ltd is accredited with the Nigeria Content Development (NCD) Department for Petroleum Resources (DPR) and the Nigeria Oil & gas Quality Control.

HSE POLICY

HEALTH, SAFETY, and ENVIRONMENT POLICY


Prioriserv Consults Limited is committed to providing and maintaining a safe and healthy workplace for all staff, reducing any impact operations have on the environment, and providing the information, training, and supervision to all staff and contractors needed to achieve this.

Whilst Prioriserv Consults Ltd has overall responsibility for health, safety & environmental procedures; employees are aware of their responsibilities and comply with the company health, safety & environment policies and procedures.

Each employee is encouraged to play a vital and responsible role in maintaining a safe and healthy workplace and caring for our environment through:

  • Being involved in the workplace health, safety & environment system.
  • Sticking to correct procedures and equipment use.
  • Wearing protective clothing and equipment as and when required.
  • Reporting any pain or discomfort as soon as possible.
  • Ensuring all accidents and incidents are reported immediately.
  • Helping new employees, trainees and visitors to the workplace understand the right procedures and why they exist.
  • Telling your manager immediately of any health, safety, or environmental concerns.
  • Keeping the workplace tidy to minimize the risk of any accidents or mishaps

    • This policy says how Prioriserv Consults Ltd manages its health, safety & environmental responsibilities.


    Prioriserv manages health, safety & environmental policy by:

  • Controlling our health and safety risks at work.
  • Involving employees on health, safety & environmental issues that affect them.
  • Making sure that our employees work in a safe environment and that any equipment they use is safe.
  • Making sure dangerous substances are stored, used, and disposed of in a safe and environmentally friendly manner.
  • Making sure employees, especially new employees, have relevant information and training on health, safety & environmental policy.
  • Making sure employees can do their jobs and are properly trained.
  • We stop accidents and work-related health problems.
  • Regularly checking that working conditions are safe and healthy.
  • Regularly review our policy and make changes if necessary.
